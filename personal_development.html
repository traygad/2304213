Reflection on "Twelve Pillars" (Chapters 4-7)

The writers of *Twelve Pillars* address the value of connections, leadership, and personal development in Chapters 4–7. The importance of surrounding oneself with the proper people who challenge and encourage you is one of the most important lessons I learned from these chapters. I want to use this in my life, especially as I pursue my academic and professional goals..

Another significant lesson is that success is not just about personal achievement but also about helping others grow. This idea resonates with me, and I intend to incorporate it into my future career as a web developer, where collaboration and teamwork will be key to success.
